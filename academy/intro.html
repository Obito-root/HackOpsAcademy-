🛡️ Chapter 1: Introduction to Ethical Hacking

Welcome to the world of hacking. In this chapter, you’ll learn the difference between:

  • Black Hat Hackers – Malicious hackers who exploit systems illegally.
  • White Hat Hackers – Ethical hackers who test systems for security flaws.
  • Grey Hat Hackers – Operate in the middle, often hacking without permission but with good intent.

💡 Why Ethical Hacking Matters

Ethical hacking helps organizations protect their data, fix vulnerabilities, and stay secure in the digital world.
